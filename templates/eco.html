🌍 Eco-Friendly Tips
💡 Save energy: Turn off lights and devices when not in use.
🚰 Conserve water: Fix leaks and use water-saving appliances.
🛍️ Reduce plastic: Carry reusable bags and bottles.
🚴 Go green: Walk, cycle, or use public transport when possible.
🌱 Plant trees: Support greenery and community gardening.
